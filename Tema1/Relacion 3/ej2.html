2. The Lifetime Supply Calculator: function supplyCalculator *Ever wonder how much a "lifetime supply" of your favorite snack is? Wonder no more!* - Store your current age into a variable. - Store a maximum age into a variable. - Store an estimated amount per day (as a number). - Calculate how many you would eat total for the rest of your life. - Output the result to the screen like so: "You will need NN to last you until the ripe old age of X".
